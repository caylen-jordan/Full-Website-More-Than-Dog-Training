My Story

More Than a Trainer.
A Genuine Partner
for You and Your Dog.

I've been working with dogs for over ten years — and in that time, I've worked with hundreds of dogs across every breed, age, temperament, and challenge imaginable. From brand-new puppies learning the basics to reactive adult dogs that had been written off by other trainers, I've seen it all.

I founded More Than Dog Training in 2018 in Hutto, TX with a simple belief: every dog is capable of more than their owners are being told. With the right training — and the right trainer — most behavioral challenges can be worked through. You just have to be willing to put in the work.

My specialty is off-leash obedience and reactivity. These are the two areas where I see the biggest transformation and the most life-changing results — a dog that can be trusted off-leash in the real world, or a reactive dog that finally gets to enjoy a walk without melting down. Those wins never get old.

What sets me apart isn't just the results — it's how I get there. I'm direct and honest about what's realistic, genuinely invested in every dog I work with, and committed to making sure the training sticks when your dog comes home. You won't get a polished sales pitch from me. You'll get the truth, a solid plan, and a trainer who actually shows up.

The Approach

Training That Works
In Real Life

A lot of training works great — until it doesn't. Until your dog sees a squirrel, a stranger, another dog, or a skateboard and everything goes out the window. I train for the real world, not the training field.

Every program is built around your dog's specific challenges and your specific goals. There's no off-the-shelf curriculum here. What works for one dog won't work for another, and I build every session accordingly.

I'm passionate about off-leash obedience because it represents real trust — a dog that can be off leash safely is a dog that genuinely understands what's being asked of them. That level of reliability doesn't come from shortcuts, and I don't take any.

I'm equally passionate about helping reactive dogs — not because reactivity is a quick fix, but because seeing a dog finally relax on a walk is one of the most rewarding things in this work. Both the dog and the owner get their freedom back.
