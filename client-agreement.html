Service Agreement

Client
Agreement

Please read each section carefully before signing. This agreement outlines how we work together and protects both you and your dog.

This agreement is between More Than Dog Training (owner: Caylen Jordan, Hutto, TX) and the client named below. By signing, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to all terms outlined in this document. Both owners of the dog — including spouses or partners — are required to sign.

Section 1

Safety Protocols

Client agrees to disclose any known history of aggression, reactivity, fear, or behavioral concerns prior to and throughout all training sessions. All dogs must remain on a secure leash at all times unless otherwise directed by the trainer. Client is responsible for maintaining control of their dog at all times and agrees to follow all safety instructions provided by More Than Dog Training. More Than Dog Training reserves the right to modify or end a session at any time if a safety concern arises, with no refund owed for the unused portion of that session.

Section 2

Liability

Client acknowledges that dog training involves inherent risk, including but not limited to bites, scratches, and other physical injury. Client agrees to hold More Than Dog Training and its owner harmless from any and all claims, damages, losses, or expenses — including attorney's fees — arising from injury to any person, animal, or property caused by the client's dog, whether during or outside of training sessions. Client assumes full financial and legal responsibility for any harm caused by their dog at any time.

Section 3

Vaccinations, Licensing & Identification Records

Client certifies that their dog is current on all vaccinations required by Texas law, including rabies, and is properly licensed per local ordinance. Client agrees to provide proof of vaccination records upon request. Dogs with unknown or lapsed vaccination histories may be refused service until documentation is provided. Client is responsible for ensuring all records remain current throughout the duration of training.

Section 4

Training Methods & Tools

More Than Dog Training uses balanced training methods tailored to each dog's individual temperament and needs. These methods may include positive reinforcement, marker training, leash pressure, e-collar conditioning, and other tools deemed appropriate by the trainer. Client understands and consents to the use of these methods and tools. Client agrees to communicate any concerns openly and to refrain from introducing conflicting methods or tools without first consulting with the trainer.

Section 5

Satisfaction Guarantees

Training outcomes are influenced by many factors, including the dog's temperament, history, age, and health, as well as the owner's consistency and participation outside of sessions. While More Than Dog Training is fully committed to achieving the best possible results, no specific outcome or timeline can be guaranteed. Results vary by dog and by owner. More Than Dog Training does not guarantee any particular behavior change, and no refunds will be issued based solely on unmet expectations.

Section 6

Owner Commitment

Client understands that the success of training depends significantly on their active participation between sessions. Client agrees to practice assigned exercises consistently, use the tools and methods taught by the trainer, follow all recommendations provided, and communicate openly about challenges, setbacks, or changes in their dog's behavior. Failure to maintain consistency between sessions may limit results.

Section 7

Payment

Payment is due at the time of each session unless otherwise agreed upon in writing. For packages, payment is due in full prior to the first session unless a written payment plan has been arranged. Accepted payment methods include Venmo (@Caylen-Jordan) and cash. Unpaid balances may result in the suspension of upcoming sessions until the balance is resolved.

Section 8

Cancellations & Refunds

Clients must provide a minimum of 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule a session. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice will be charged in full. For board & train programs, a non-refundable deposit is required to hold a spot, and the remaining balance is non-refundable once the dog has been dropped off. Unused package sessions may be considered for a refund at the trainer's discretion within 30 days of purchase, minus a $50 administrative fee.

Section 9

Photo & Video Release

Client grants More Than Dog Training permission to photograph and/or video record their dog — and the client, if present — during training sessions for promotional use, including but not limited to social media, website, and marketing materials. Clients who wish to opt out of this release must notify the trainer in writing before services begin. Opting out will not affect the quality of services received.
