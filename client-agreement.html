More Than Dog Training · Client Agreement & Policy Acknowledgment
Hutto, TX · Serving the Greater Austin Area

This agreement must be completed in full prior to the start of any services. Please read each section carefully before acknowledging.

Section 1
Client & Dog Information

Please complete all fields below. This information is used to ensure the safety and wellbeing of your dog throughout the training process.

Section 2
Medical Information

Please email any of the following to caylen@morethandogtraining.com prior to your start date, with your dog's name in the subject line:

Required vaccinations include Rabies, DA2PP/DHPP, and Bordetella. Leptospirosis is strongly recommended.

  • Vaccinations received within the last 6 months
  • Recent injuries or surgeries, including spay/neuter procedures
  • Chronic illnesses or ongoing health conditions
  • Current medications with dosage amounts (including antibiotics and probiotics)
  • Known allergies, whether environmental or food-related

Any dog exhibiting symptoms of illness, injury, or any contagious condition — including but not limited to sneezing, coughing, runny eyes or nose, vomiting, diarrhea, lethargy, loss of appetite, or limping — will be excused from services until a veterinarian has confirmed the dog is cleared to return.

Section 3
Behavioral History & Bite Disclosure

I warrant that, to the best of my knowledge, I have fully disclosed any and all history of aggression, reactivity, resource guarding, fear-based behavior, prior bites, or other behavioral concerns.

I acknowledge that failure to disclose a known bite history or aggressive behavior prior to the start of services constitutes a material misrepresentation. In such cases, More Than Dog Training shall bear no liability for any resulting incident, and all costs, damages, and legal responsibility remain solely with me.

Section 4
Safety Protocols

In-Home Lessons

For the safety of all parties, please ensure your dog is secured prior to your trainer's arrival. Acceptable methods include:

  • Leashed and held by a responsible adult
  • Crated
  • On "place" with a tie-back
  • In a separate room (bedroom or bathroom)

This prevents escapes out the front door and reduces risk of unexpected interactions at entry. During all in-home lessons, your dog must wear a secure, properly fitted buckle collar they cannot slip out of, and must be on a standard 6-foot leash or slip lead at all times when outside of their crate.

The following equipment is NOT permitted during training sessions:

  • Harnesses of any kind
  • Elastic or retractable ("flexi") leashes

Board & Train — Drop-Off & Pick-Up

All dogs must arrive leashed, crated, or otherwise fully secured during drop-off and pick-up handoffs. More Than Dog Training is not responsible for incidents that occur as a result of a dog being unsecured during transport or transfer.

Section 6
Training Methods & Tools

More Than Dog Training uses a balanced, results-based approach to training. Depending on the dog and program, training tools may include, but are not limited to:

  • Flat buckle collar
  • Prong collar
  • E-collar / remote training collar
  • Slip lead
  • Martingale collar
  • Dominant dog collar
  • Head halti
  • Transitional leash
  • Pet convincer
  • Muzzle
  • "Bonker" (rolled towel used as a sound interrupter)

I acknowledge I have had the opportunity to ask questions about any of these tools prior to signing and agree to their safe, responsible use throughout the training program.

Section 7
Satisfaction Guarantee

More Than Dog Training is committed to doing everything within its power to help you and your dog reach your goals. Your trainer will be on time and will communicate in advance if running late.

However, More Than Dog Training cannot guarantee specific behavioral outcomes once the dog is in my care, as the long-term success of any training program depends on my ongoing commitment. Training is a team effort between trainer, dog, and owner — and results are directly tied to consistency of practice.

Section 8
Owner Commitment

I acknowledge and agree that the success of any training program — whether private lessons, board and train, or virtual — depends on the combined effort of both myself and my dog. More Than Dog Training will provide education through in-person instruction, scheduled sessions, and ongoing updates via video, audio messages, calls, written materials, or other media as appropriate to the program.

I understand and agree to the following:

  • A dog's behavior is not static. Even well-trained behaviors require consistent practice and reinforcement. No dog is ever considered "cured," though behavior can be modified and managed, including in cases involving aggression, reactivity, fear, or anxiety.
  • Long-term success requires my full compliance with the trainer's instructions, protocols, and recommended tools. Training must be practiced and maintained as a daily lifestyle, not treated as a one-time fix.
  • I am responsible for replicating and maintaining the training environment, structure, and techniques provided by the trainer, both during and after the program. Failure to do so may result in behavioral regression. No guarantee of permanent results is made if I do not uphold these responsibilities.
  • All decision-making adults in the household must be willing to participate in and support the training program. Children may attend sessions if mature enough to receive and follow instructions.
  • I agree to be on time, have all training equipment ready, and complete any assigned homework prior to each lesson.
  • If my inability or unwillingness to practice becomes a recurring issue, More Than Dog Training reserves the right to discuss alternative options or terminate the agreement.

Board & Train Safety

If a dog's behavior escalates to a level that poses a safety risk to the trainer, other dogs, or household members at any point during a board and train program, More Than Dog Training reserves the right to contact the Client and require early pickup. In such cases, a partial refund may be issued at the trainer's discretion, based on the number of days of service completed.

Section 10
Cancellations, No-Shows & Refunds

Cancelling or Rescheduling

All cancellations and reschedules must be made at least 48 hours in advance of the scheduled appointment. Each program allows a maximum of 2 reschedules, regardless of notice given. Sessions cancelled or rescheduled beyond that limit will be forfeited without refund.

Program completion timelines:

  • 6-week programs must be completed within 8 weeks of the start date
  • 8-week programs must be completed within 10 weeks of the start date

No-Shows

Failure to appear for a scheduled session without any prior notice constitutes a no-show. No-shows will be counted as a forfeited session and will not be rescheduled or refunded.

Termination of Service

If I choose to discontinue service before completing 50% of the program (measured by number of sessions or board and train days), a partial, prorated refund will be issued. No refunds will be granted after 50% of the program has been completed.

More Than Dog Training reserves the right to terminate services at any time if I or any household member is not in compliance with training protocols, instructions, or the terms of this agreement. In such cases, a partial refund may be issued at the trainer's discretion.

Digital Products & Online Courses

Due to the digital nature of online courses and electronically delivered materials, all sales are final. No refunds will be issued for any digital products once delivered.

If I cannot be reached within a reasonable timeframe and emergency care is required, More Than Dog Training is authorized to seek emergency veterinary treatment on my dog's behalf.

I agree to assume full financial responsibility for all resulting veterinary costs, including emergency care, diagnostics, and treatment. More Than Dog Training will not be held financially liable for any veterinary expenses incurred on my behalf.

Section 12 — Informational Only
Communication & Progress Updates

More Than Dog Training is committed to keeping you informed and involved throughout your dog's time in our care. The following communication standards apply:

Board & Train

Clients enrolled in a board and train program will receive progress updates several times per week via video, photo, or voice message. Updates will not be provided on a daily basis and will not be sent on Sundays. Please know that no news is good news — if you do not hear from us, your dog is doing well and progressing as expected. In the event of any change in your dog's health, behavior, or wellbeing while in our care, you will be contacted promptly and directly.

Boarding

Boarding clients will receive updates every other day via video, photo, or voice message. Updates will not be sent on Sundays. As with board and train, no news is good news — any changes in your dog's health or wellbeing will be communicated to you immediately.

General Communication

  • All client communications will be conducted via text, email, phone, or WhatsApp as agreed at intake.
  • More Than Dog Training will make reasonable efforts to respond to client inquiries within 24–48 business hours.

I agree to remain accessible and responsive throughout the duration of my program, particularly during board and train or boarding stays, when timely communication regarding my dog's health or behavior may be necessary.
