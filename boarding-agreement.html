Client & Dog Information

Section 2
Medical Information & Vaccinations

Please email any of the following to caylen@morethandogtraining.com prior to your dog's stay, with your dog's name in the subject line:

Required vaccinations include Rabies, DA2PP/DHPP, and Bordetella. Leptospirosis is strongly recommended.

  • Vaccinations received within the last 6 months
  • Recent injuries or surgeries
  • Chronic illnesses or ongoing health conditions
  • Current medications with dosage amounts (including antibiotics and probiotics)
  • Known allergies, whether environmental or food-related

All vaccination records must be on file no less than 48 hours before the first day of boarding. More Than Dog Training will not admit any dog without all required records on file.

Any dog exhibiting symptoms of illness, injury, or any contagious condition — including but not limited to sneezing, coughing, runny eyes or nose, vomiting, diarrhea, lethargy, loss of appetite, or limping — will be excused from services until a veterinarian has confirmed the dog is cleared to return.

Section 3
Behavioral History & Bite Disclosure

I warrant that, to the best of my knowledge, I have fully disclosed any and all history of aggression, reactivity, resource guarding, fear-based behavior, prior bites, or other behavioral concerns.

Failure to disclose a known bite history or aggressive behavior prior to boarding constitutes a material misrepresentation. In such cases, More Than Dog Training shall bear no liability for any resulting incident, and all costs, damages, and legal responsibility remain solely with the Client.

Section 4
Drop-Off & Pick-Up Protocols

All dogs must arrive leashed, crated, or otherwise fully secured during drop-off and pick-up handoffs. More Than Dog Training is not responsible for incidents that occur as a result of a dog being unsecured during transport or transfer.

Drop-off and pick-up times will be confirmed at the time of booking. Please do not arrive outside of the agreed window without prior notice.

Section 5
Liability Release

More Than Dog Training takes every reasonable precaution to ensure a safe environment. However, due to the inherently unpredictable nature of animal behavior, no boarding environment can be guaranteed entirely risk-free.

By signing below, I (on behalf of myself and any individuals in my household) agree to release and hold harmless More Than Dog Training, its owner, assistants, volunteers, and any participating property owners from all liability, claims, damages, illness, injury, or death that may arise directly or indirectly — now or in the future — as a result of participation in any service provided by More Than Dog Training.

I assume full financial and legal responsibility for any damage, injury, illness, or death caused by my dog during the course of the boarding stay.

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas. Any disputes arising from this agreement shall first be addressed through good-faith negotiation. If unresolved, both parties agree to pursue binding mediation in Williamson County, Texas prior to initiating any legal action.

Section 6
Payment & Deposit

A non-refundable deposit is required to reserve a boarding slot. The remaining balance is due in full on or before the first day of boarding. Deposit amount will be communicated at time of booking.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Venmo
  • Check (made payable to More Than Dog Training)
  • Cash

A $35 fee will be assessed for any returned or bounced check. More Than Dog Training reserves the right to suspend services for any account with an outstanding unpaid balance.

Section 7
Cancellations & Refunds

All cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance of the scheduled drop-off date. The non-refundable deposit will be forfeited for any cancellation regardless of notice given.

The remaining balance (beyond the deposit) will be refunded in full if cancelled with at least 48 hours' notice. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours' notice will be charged in full.

If a dog's behavior escalates to a level that poses a safety risk to the trainer, other dogs, or household members during the boarding stay, More Than Dog Training reserves the right to contact the Client and require early pickup. In such cases, a partial refund may be issued at the trainer's discretion based on the number of days completed.

Section 8
Emergency Veterinary Authorization

In the event of a veterinary emergency, More Than Dog Training will make every reasonable effort to contact me immediately. If I cannot be reached within a reasonable timeframe and emergency care is required, More Than Dog Training is authorized to seek emergency veterinary treatment on my dog's behalf.

I agree to assume full financial responsibility for all resulting veterinary costs. More Than Dog Training will not be held financially liable for any veterinary expenses incurred on my behalf.

Section 9 Optional
Photo & Video Release

More Than Dog Training may photograph and/or video record your dog during their stay for the purpose of progress updates, social media, and marketing materials. Any media shared with you is the private property of More Than Dog Training and is intended solely for your personal use.

This section is optional. Declining will not affect your dog's care.

Section 10
Final Acknowledgment

By signing below, I certify that I am 18 years of age or older, am the legal owner of the dog or have been granted full authority by the legal owner, and have read this agreement in its entirety. All information provided is accurate and complete, and I fully understand and agree to all terms set forth herein.
