Every program is tailored to your dog. No cookie-cutter plans, no pressure. Just honest training that works — in real life, not just in a controlled setting.

Most Popular

Board & Train —
4 Weeks, Full Immersion

Lives with Caylen · Hutto, TX

This is the deep end. Your dog comes to live with Caylen — in her home, not a typical, noisy kennel — for four full weeks of focused, daily training. They experience a calm, home environment, go on outings, and work through everything that matters to you.

The result? Dogs that come home with rock-solid obedience, reliable off-leash recall, and the ability to stay calm and focused in real-world situations — busy parks, patios, trails, crowds. Not just in a training field.

The program wraps with a full handoff session where Caylen walks you through exactly how your dog has been trained, so you can maintain everything they've learned. You're not left on your own.

Private Training —
Focused, One-on-One

At Caylen's Location · Hutto, TX

Private training sessions begin at Caylen's location in Hutto, TX, with the option of meeting at another nearby location to practice what your dog learns in real-world environments to work through distractions, not just perform in a sterile setting.

Whether you're working on basic obedience, leash manners, recall, reactivity, or building on existing skills, sessions are completely customized to where your dog is right now and where you want them to go.

You'll be hands-on throughout the process, learning how to communicate clearly with your dog so the training sticks long after the session ends.
