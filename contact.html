Quick Answers

Common Questions

How does the free call work?

It's a 30-minute conversation — no sales pitch, just a chance to talk about your dog and your goals. Caylen will ask some questions, share her honest assessment, and let you know which program she'd recommend. You can book it directly here.

Do you share pricing on the website?

No — and intentionally so. Every dog is different, and pricing reflects the specific plan Caylen builds for your situation. You'll get full pricing details on your free call after she understands what your dog needs.

I'm not local to Hutto. Can I still work with you?

Yes. Virtual training is available for clients anywhere. Some families also travel for the board & train program — it's worth a conversation to see what makes sense for you.

My dog is really reactive. Is that too much for board & train?

Reactivity is one of Caylen's specialties — not a dealbreaker. Some of the most dramatic transformations she's seen have been with reactive dogs. Reach out and let's talk about what you're dealing with.

How long until I hear back after submitting the form?

Caylen personally reviews every inquiry and typically responds within 1–2 business days. If you need something faster, book a call directly — that's the quickest path to a conversation.
